OUR OUTSTANDING SERVICES

CalPhos Enterprises provide Consultancy services / Basic Engineering for Ossein, Gelatine, Phosphoric Acid, Di-Calcium Phosphate, Single Super Phosphate, Conversion of Low Grade Rock Phosphate to Di-Calcium Phosphate and Phosphoric Acid and Fluro Silicic Acid Treatment. We have offered our Technology to India, Malaysia, Iran, Turkey and Egypt.


CalPhos Enterprises also provide Technical Services for P2O5 recovery, Capacity Enhancement, Cost reduction in Existing Phosphate Industries. In addition, Conversion of Low Grade Rock Phosphate and Fluro Silicic Acid into value added Products.CalPhos is involved in Technology Supply, Manufacturing, Export & Import & Distribution for past Two Decades in the field of Phosphatic Products, Ossein, Gelatin, Collagen & Keratin, Essential Oils, Herbal Products, Spices, and Agri Cultural Products.
