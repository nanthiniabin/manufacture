ABOUT CALPHOS ENTERPRICES

CalPhos Enterprises, based in India, specializes in engineering solutions and provides technology for transforming rejected low-grade rock phosphate (15-22% P2O5) into phosphatic products, along with producing fluoro silicic acid for SSF & di-calcium phosphate.


For the past two decades, CalPhos has been engaged in technology supply, manufacturing, export, import, and distribution in the field of phosphatic products, ossein, gelatin, collagen, keratin, essential oils, herbal products, spices, and agricultural products.

Consultancy services / Basic Engineering

CalPhos Enterprises offers consultancy and engineering services for various industries, including ossein, gelatine, and phosphoric acid production. We specialize in converting low-grade rock phosphate into valuable products and provide fluoro silicic acid treatment. Our expertise extends globally, with projects in India, Malaysia, Iran, Turkey, and Egypt.

Technical Services for P2O5 Recovery

CalPhos Enterprises is a premier provider of specialized technical services tailored for the phosphate industry, emphasizing P2O5 recovery, capacity enhancement, and cost reduction. Our proficiency extends to transforming low-grade rock phosphate and fluoro silicic acid into valuable products. Through innovative solutions, we champion efficiency and sustainability within the phosphate sector.
